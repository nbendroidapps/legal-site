Who we are

Islamic Literature Library (“the App”) provides access to Islamic books for reading. Free users see ads; Premium subscriptions remove ads and unlock unlimited reading. The App is published by Md Mamunur Rasid (“we”, “us”). Country of operation: Bangladesh.

Information we collect

  • Account & authentication. If you sign in (e.g., Sign in with Apple via Firebase Auth), we receive identifiers provided by Apple and/or Firebase (an app-scoped user ID and, if you choose to share it, name and email).
  • App data you create. Bookmarks, last-read position, preferences. Stored on your device and/or our app database for your account.
  • Diagnostics & performance (optional). Crash and usage events to keep the app reliable (e.g., Firebase services, if enabled).
  • Ads & device info (free tier). For free users we show ads using Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). AdMob and its partners may collect device identifiers, approximate location, and interaction data to serve and measure ads.

We do not sell personal data.

How we use information

  • Provide core features (sign-in, library access, bookmarks, reading position).
  • Process subscriptions and entitlements (via Apple).
  • Show ads to free users (via AdMob).
  • Diagnose issues and improve the App.

Sharing

  • Service providers: Firebase (Auth/other), Google Mobile Ads, analytics/diagnostics vendors.
  • Apple: All purchases and subscription management run through Apple’s systems.

We do not otherwise share personal data except when required by law or with your consent.

Your choices

  • Tracking & personalized ads (free tier): On iOS open Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Tracking to control app tracking, or reset your Advertising Identifier. You can also adjust Apple Advertising settings in Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸ Apple Advertising.
  • Access/Deletion: Email us at bendroidappsn@gmail.com or message WhatsApp +971 56 774 3842 and we’ll help with access or deletion requests.
  • Subscriptions: Manage or cancel in Settings ▸ Apple ID ▸ Subscriptions.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. You may request deletion via the contact below.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International users

Your data may be processed in countries where our providers operate. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will update the “Effective date” above and, if changes are material, provide in-app notice.
